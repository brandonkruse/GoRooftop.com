ABOUT ROOFTOP

Read about our team, mission, culture and more below. Enjoy!

RoofTop has been built to create a simplified automation broadcast network. Our headquarters is located in Huntsville, Alabama. Our mission is to empower those who need a reliable tool to broadcast their message locally or even worldwide. Over the past years we have strategically put together an amazing team with a great attitude and extremely passionate. Rest assured, you are in good hands!

Founder / CEO - Brandon Kruse

Mr. Kruse has always been fascinated by Technology and Telecom attending Huntsville's HSV2600 meetings and working as a Software Developer for Digium Inc., a Huntsville-based communication technology company. In 2008 Mr. Kruse recognized the need for a higher level of integration and support for smaller telecommunication companies and started Dialmaxx. His company was successful bridging the gap between service providers and customers with a fully redundant, multi-geographic failover and load-balanced tier-1 termination network. Dialmaxx became the provider of VoIP telephony services and infrastructure design, with over 9 million phone lines/subscribers. In 2012 Dialmaxx sold to YMAX Communications and Mr. Kruse started his next ventures and launched several high-tech and communication companies specializing in Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP), Asterisk, Session Initiation Protocol (SIP), and Networking.
